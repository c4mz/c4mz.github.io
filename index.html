Something different,
Something delicious.

Conveniently situated at the entrance to Maleny's hinterland, this quaint restaurant with its eclectic Old World charm offers something unique for everyone.

Become a Gypsy for the night and ignite your sense of adventure with our creative Global menu! Selections of quality steaks, exotic seafood, unusual game dishes & authentic curries...the list is endless and ever-changing.
Hand-carved, leather-bound menus tempt you to a comprehensive array of boutique Australian & carefully sourced World Wines. A separate innovative Vegetarian & Vegan Menu is available on request.
